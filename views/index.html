What is Catfacts.io?

Catfacts.io was a fun project I made to trial certain tools, and is based on the catfacts pranks that was popular while back. Only this implementation is less spammy, and you can unsubscribe at any time.

What happens when I subscribe?

When you enter your number in the field above you will be sent a confirmation text asking if it was really you that subscribed your own phone number. You can confirm or deny this by responding with Y or N.

Can I unsubscribe?

Once you subscribe you are told that, you can unsubscribe at any time by texting Q to catfacts. Once you text this, your number is removed from catfacts and you will no longer receive catfacts.

How can I trust Catfacts?

Catfacts is open source, meaning all it's code is freely avaible online for both use, extension, miroring, criticism, and whatever else. But most importantly it means catfacts acts transparently so you can be sure we aren't doing any thing malicious with your phone number.
