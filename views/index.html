What is Catfacts.io?

Catfacts.io was a fun project I made to trial certain tools, and is based on the catfacts pranks that was popular while back. Only this implementation is less spammy, and you can unsubscribe at any time.

Can I unsubscribe?

Once you subscribe you are told that, you can unsubscribe at any time by texting Q to catfacts. Once you text this, your number is removed from catfacts and you will no longer receive catfacts.

Can I trust Catfacts?

Catfacts is open source (MIT), meaning all it's code is freely avaible online for free use, extension, mirroring, critique, and whatever else. But most importantly it means catfacts is transparent in a way that you can be sure it's not doing any thing malicious with your phone number, if you have any suspicions. Just remember this isn't a serious project, so I don't plan take responiblity for any incidents, so be smart.

What happens when I subscribe?

When you enter your number in the field above you will be sent a confirmation text asking if it was really you that subscribed your own phone number. You can confirm or deny this by responding with Y or N. So you don't have to worry about being signed up without being aware of it, this also means if you decide subscribe your friend they will be notified.
